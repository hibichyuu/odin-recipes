Rice

nice, sticky, hot & steamy bowl of rice

This recipe will show you how to make a PERFECT bowl of Rice (not really)

Ingredients

    1 & half cups of rice 2 cups of water

Steps

    put 2 cups of water in small pot put 1 & half cups of rice in small pot bring to consistent boil immediatly put the heat on low for a rolling boil wait 20 minutes for rice to absorb water
