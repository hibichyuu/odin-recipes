Ramen Noodles

delicious store bought ramen noodles

This recipe is a step by step process on how to make an affordable tongue heaven

Ingredients

    2 store bought ramen noodles packets 3 cups of water boiled eggs (optional) chopped green onions (optional)

Steps

    Boil 3 cups of water in medium pot put dry noodles from both packets into pot stir ramen noodles with fork until noodles seperate drain water from pot put ramen noodles in bowl (optional) add 2 halves of a boiled egg on top (optional) add a handful of chopped green onions (optional)
